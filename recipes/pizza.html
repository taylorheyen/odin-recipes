Pizza Recipe!

Description

The ultimate food item. Pizza has brought the world together, and it will forever bring the world together. Pizza is the food of the people.

What is better than learninh how to make the food of the people!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Crust
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozz Cheese
  • Toppings of Choice(Plain Cheese for authenticity)

Steps

  1. Put Tomato Sauce on Pizza Crust
  2. Spread a heavy layer of Mozz cheese on top of tomato sauce
  3. Apply toppings of choice onto cheese
  4. Bake for an appropriate amount of time
